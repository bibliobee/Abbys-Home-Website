Screen Shot 2021-07-20 at 9.45.53 AM.png Threading Change artist fundraiser featureMay 2021 I was one of several artists featured in a fundraiser in May 2021 by Threading Change for Asian Heritage Month. I read an excerpt of one of my short stories, Buko, about Filipino diasporic identity explored through cultural cuisines, with a sprinkle of romance.
The fundraiser proceeds benefitted organizations that advance anti-racism policy, build educational capacity and increase the visibility of Asian artists and change-makers in the community.
