I’m Abby, a writer and creative entrepreneur.
I write about food, Filipino diaspora, and queer and platonic love from my home in Vancouver, Canada. My work is published in the emerge22 anthology by Simon Fraser University, and I’ve been honoured to have read my work at the 2022 Vancouver Writer’s Fest.
I am a scholarship recipient of the 2022 Whistler Writer in Residence mentorship. Look for my forthcoming fiction in Room Magazine and Hungry Zine.
Stay tuned- I’m thinking of ways to engage with my communities on my website. Updates to come soon!
